LifeVault Hub Support

Need help with your account, documents, password recovery, cloud backup or another feature? Contact us and we will respond as soon as possible.

Support email:
lifevaulthub.support@gmail.com

Typical response time: within 24–48 hours.

Common help topics

  • Verification email not received: Check Inbox, Spam, Promotions and All Mail, then request a new code after the countdown ends.
  • Forgotten password: Use “Forgot Password” on the sign-in screen.
  • Cloud backup: Sign in with the same account used to create the backup.
  • Report a bug: Include your device model, iOS version and what happened.

Delete account or data

Email us from the address linked to your account and use the subject “Account Deletion Request”.
